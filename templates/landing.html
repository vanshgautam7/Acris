How it works

Three steps from resume to results

01
📄

Upload your resume

Drop your PDF resume. pdfplumber extracts and cleans all text content automatically — no formatting needed.

02
🧠

Skills are extracted

A four-pass NLP engine using spaCy PhraseMatcher and rule-based fallbacks identifies every skill in your resume.

03
🔗

Queries are generated

Role-filtered skills become optimised search URLs for LinkedIn, Indeed, and Naukri — ready to click.

Features

Built for candidates, not recruiters

⚡

Hybrid skill extraction

Combines spaCy phrase matching with exact, token-based, and joined-word detection to catch every skill regardless of how it's written.

🎯

Role-aware filtering

Extracted skills are filtered against 21 job-role mappings so your queries only include what employers actually look for.

📊

Live job matching

Real job listings fetched via SerpAPI and ranked by a match score calculated from your role and filtered skill set.

🔔

Email notifications

n8n workflow automation delivers matched job listings to your inbox whenever your profile is updated or jobs are refreshed.

🔐

Secure by default

Google OAuth 2.0 login, bcrypt password hashing, session-based auth, and parameterised SQL queries on every database call. Your data stays yours.

Tech stack

Open source, end to end

Built on Python and FastAPI with a modular pipeline architecture that supports progressive extension across future phases.

Python 3 FastAPI spaCy pdfplumber SQLAlchemy SQLite SerpAPI n8n Google OAuth 2.0 APScheduler Jinja2 Uvicorn
